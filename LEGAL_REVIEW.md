# Legal Wording Review

This file tracks wording that should be reviewed before broad public promotion.

- `data/obligations.json`: descriptions and assessment items were extracted from the current Directive Zero v1 tool without rewriting.
- `data/scope.json`: statutory anchors and classification explanations were extracted without rewriting.
- `data/deadlines.json`: deadline labels are factual summaries of statutory clocks.

Review goal: keep statutory facts precise, clearly attributed, and written in Directive Zero's own explanatory wording. Do not imply authorship of legal obligations.
